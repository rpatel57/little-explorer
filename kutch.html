Kutch Desert

Kutch district, officially spelled Kachchh, is a district of Gujarat state in western India, with its headquarters (capital) at Bhuj. Covering an area of 45,674 km2, it is the largest district of India. The Kutch district is home to the Kutchi people who speak the Kutchi language.
